The Best Movies According to Kavya

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

It sparkles with romance, merriment, and heartache, boasting crackling chemistry between its lead actors.

Jab We Met

Is one of the most refreshing love stories made in recent times. A simple, interesting, and charming film about a heart-broken and serious guy who meets an extremely vivacious and talkative girl, gradually changing his perception of life and love.

3 Idiots

3 Idiots not only entertains but also enlightens. It is forward-thinking, promises lots of laughs, and delivers a heartwarming message. A movie that remains etched in memory with immense recall value.

"Movies have the power to inspire, entertain, and change the way we see the world."
